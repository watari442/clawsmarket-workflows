# Lead Scoring Rubric

Score each lead on a 0-100 scale across four dimensions:

## Company Fit (0-25)
- 25: Perfect size + industry match
- 20: Right industry, slightly off on size
- 15: Adjacent industry, right size
- 10: Partial match on both
- 0-5: Poor fit

## Title/Seniority (0-25)
- 25: Decision maker (C-suite, VP, Head of)
- 20: Strong influencer (Director, Sr. Manager)
- 15: Manager level
- 10: Individual contributor with budget influence
- 0-5: No buying authority

## Timing Signals (0-25)
- 25: Active buying signal (RFP, demo request, competitor switch)
- 20: Strong signal (recent funding, rapid hiring in relevant dept)
- 15: Moderate signal (new product launch, expansion)
- 10: Weak signal (industry event attendance, content engagement)
- 0-5: No signals

## Personalization Opportunity (0-25)
- 25: Rich public info (recent interview, blog post, specific pain mentioned)
- 20: Good company news to reference
- 15: LinkedIn activity or conference talk
- 10: Basic company info available
- 0-5: No personalization hooks found

## Thresholds
- 80+: High priority — draft email immediately
- 60-79: Medium priority — draft email, batch send
- 40-59: Low priority — add to nurture list
- <40: Skip — does not meet ICP
