# Outreach Email Template

Write a cold outreach email with these constraints:

## Format
- Subject: specific to their situation, NOT generic sales language
- Body: under 150 words
- Opening: reference something specific about their company (recent news, product, challenge)
- Value prop: one clear sentence about how you can help
- CTA: soft ask (quick call, reply with thoughts) — NOT hard close
- Sign-off: casual and professional

## Anti-patterns (NEVER do these)
- "I hope this email finds you well"
- "I noticed your company is growing"
- "Let me know if you're interested"
- ALL CAPS or excessive exclamation marks
- More than one CTA
- Attachments or links in first email
- Mentioning competitors by name

## Good example
Subject: Saw your Series A — quick thought on [specific challenge]

Hi [Name],

Congrats on the raise. Noticed you're hiring 3 engineers — scaling the team while shipping fast is brutal.

We built [specific thing] that helps teams like yours [specific outcome]. [Customer name] cut their [metric] by [number] after switching.

Worth a 15-min call to see if it fits?

[Signature]
